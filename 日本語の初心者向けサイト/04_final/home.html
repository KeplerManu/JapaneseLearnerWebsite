Learn Japanese!

Welcome to Japanese: The Basics! This website is perfect for you if you would like to start learning Japanese from scratch, or alternatively, solidify your basic knowledge of the language.

Why choose this site?

This educational website gives you the tools to learn how to read the basic japanese alphabets, write basic kanji and construct basic sentence patterns! By the end of the site you should be able to have a good understanding of the foundational knowledge of Japanese which you can build upon to become a multilingual master. You may even be able to have a basic conversation with a Japanese person!
